About Us.

Two Developers. One City.

Anthony & Hydeia created this project with inspiration from Rinbot and with our love for anime and gaming. We are both based in NYC and developed this project as a group assignment for our fellowship. You can use pages on the navigation bar to access the how to play and start game pages. This project is also accessible on mobile, tablet and any smart devices. Feel free to connect with any of us on linkedin or check out our githubs (accessible through github logo img) for more.
